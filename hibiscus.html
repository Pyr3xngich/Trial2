Hibiscus Flavor

Our Hibiscus flavor combines the tangy, floral notes of hibiscus flowers with our premium khat extract for a uniquely refreshing and invigorating experience. The deep ruby color and distinctive taste make this a standout in our collection.

We use organically grown hibiscus flowers, carefully dried and steeped to extract their full flavor and beneficial compounds. The natural tartness of hibiscus creates a perfect counterpoint to the herbal qualities of khat, resulting in a complex and satisfying beverage.
