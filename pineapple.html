Pineapple Flavor

Our Pineapple flavor combines the tropical sweetness of ripe pineapples with our premium khat extract for a refreshing and energizing experience. Each sip delivers a burst of sunshine and natural vitality.

We use only the juiciest Kenyan pineapples, carefully selected at peak ripeness to ensure the perfect balance of sweetness and tanginess. The natural enzymes in pineapple complement the stimulating properties of khat for a truly unique beverage.

KSh 500 per bottle

Benefits:

  • Natural energy boost from khat extract
  • Rich in vitamin C and antioxidants
  • Contains bromelain, an enzyme that aids digestion
  • Helps reduce inflammation
  • Improves mental alertness and mood
  • Hydrating and refreshing, perfect for hot days

Our Pineapple flavor is particularly popular during summer months and pairs wonderfully with spicy foods. It's also an excellent mixer for creating unique tropical cocktails.
