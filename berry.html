Berry Flavor

Our Berry flavor is a delightful blend of mixed berries combined with our signature khat extract, creating a vibrant, antioxidant-rich beverage that's both energizing and delicious. This deep purple elixir offers a perfect balance of sweet and tart notes.

We use a carefully selected mix of blackberries, blueberries, and strawberries to create a complex flavor profile that complements the herbal qualities of khat. Each batch is crafted to ensure maximum flavor and nutritional benefits.

Our Berry flavor is a favorite among fitness enthusiasts and those looking for a natural pre-workout boost. The combination of berries' natural sugars and khat's energizing properties makes this an ideal choice for sustained energy without the crash associated with artificial stimulants.
